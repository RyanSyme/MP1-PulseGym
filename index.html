About Us

At Pulse Gym, you'll find everything you need for a great workout. From quality gym equipment and low cost flexible memberships, to great regular excersie and weight training classes, there are lots of reasons why you should join Pulse Gym today!.

Fitness Classes

Cardio

Zumba

Zumba is a combination of salsa dancing and aerobics which gives you a full body work out. Our Zumba class involves fast and slow dancing working the whole body for a great all-round work out.

Book Class
Step

Step is a rhythm based class on a short raised platform that you step up and down on in different patterns. Our step classes are a really fun way to get you moving and to improve your cardio fitness.

Book Class
Spinning

Spinning is a stationary cycle class that combines relies fast-paced cycling and slower paced rest intervals allowing you time to recover for the next speed burst.

Book Class

Mind and Body

Pilates

Pilates, similar to Yoga, is good for the whole body. It’s wide range of benefits include increased flexibility, more energy, better posture and reduced stress.

Book Class
Yoga

Yoga, similar to Pilates, but focuses more on breathing techniques, strength and flexibility, relaxation and mindfulness. It can help reduce stress levels and improve overall health.

Book Class
Body Tone

Body tone class is great for anyone looking to tone their body (It’s right there in the title!). The exercises are set to a soundtrack that will inspire you to a full body workout.

Book Class

Strength

Kettlebell

Kettlebell training improves strength, balance, cardio and endurance. It’s an excellent core work out that only need you and a kettlebell.

Book Class
Pump Iron

Pump Iron is a barbell workout that uses light weights and high reps. The class mixes a variety of exercises including chest presses, deadlifts, curls, squats and overhead presses.

Book Class
Killer Abs

Killer Abs is a great workout for core strengthening. It concentrates on abdominal and core exercises to tone and strengthen this area, helping to improve sports and gym performance.
